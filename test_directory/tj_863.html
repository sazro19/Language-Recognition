LocknCharge Debuts 2 Modular Carts for iPads

  • By Joshua Bolkan
  • 09/23/14

LocknCharge has launched a pair of syncing and charging carts designed for iPads, iPad Airs and iPad Minis. The iQ 30 can hold 30 of the devices and the EVO 40 can hold 40.

"Both carts are modular and mobile featuring LocknCharge’s removable iQ 16 Sync Charge Boxes, allowing syncing and charging to be done almost anywhere, independently of the cart," according to a news release. "The iQ 16 Sync Charge Box has an intelligent power management system that automatically stages the availability of power to each port. This function prevents the potential of overloading the circuit, protecting the school’s devices."


Both carts feature wells, designed to be accessible to multiple users at a time, with "ergonomic carry baskets" that hold up to five devices each to ease and speed distribution of the tools.

The iQ 30 retails for $2,999, with volume discounts available. The EVO 40 sells for $3,499. Both carts come with lifetime warranties.
