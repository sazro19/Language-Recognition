Aerohive to Showcase and Demonstrate New Products at ISTE

  • By Richard Chang
  • 06/22/16

Aerohive Networks, a wireless, cloud networking platform company, is showcasing new products at ISTE 2016 in Denver, June 26-29.

Aerohive’s HiveManager NG is a cloud-based management platform. It helps connect tablets, laptops, smart boards, online curricula and collaboration tools online and between teachers and students.

Aerohive will also display its latest 802.11ac Wave 2 access points, which together with HiveManager NG and Aerohive SR2208P, SR2224P and SR 2348P switches deliver the most adaptable cloud-enabled network infrastructure, Aerohive said in a statement.

The Milpitas, CA-based company will also provide demonstrations of an upgraded version of HiveSchool, a connected learning application that helps teachers use classroom technologies to provide engaging, collaborative and effective learning experiences for students, Aerohive said. HiveSchool’s simple deployment and management minimizes incremental IT workloads, the company said in a statement.


Last week, Aerohive unveiled a new AdvantageMSP program to enable its channel partners to deliver fully managed enterprise wired and wireless services. The new program allows customers to rely on Aerohive partners to deliver the basics of connectivity, relieving them from the burden of managing their own WiFi networks.

Aerohive will be demonstrating its products at Booth No. 2941 at ISTE. For more information, visit the company’s website or call 408-510-6100.
