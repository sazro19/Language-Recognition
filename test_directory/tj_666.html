LiteManager Adds Screen Sharing, Support for Linux, Mac OS

  • By Joshua Bolkan
  • 06/08/15

LiteManager has updated its remote support and classroom management tool, also dubbed LiteManager, to include screen sharing and support for Mac OS and Linux.

The new screen sharing feature is designed to deliver presentations, organize group projects or to provide real-time training. It includes the ability to draw on the screen and save presentations for later review.

LiteManager is available for 30 computers for free. The full version can be licensed for $7-$10.
